Is there a difference between climate change and global warming?

Global warming refers to the overall, long-term warming of the planet over the last several decades. However climate change encompasses global warming and also refers to the other violently changing elements in nature esp. natural disaters (wildfires, hurricanes etc.), melting glaciers, and accelerating ice melts in the Arcitc and Antarctic.
